RAAR FASHIONS is a family run boutique launched to cater overseas customers with tastefully designed traditional Indian dresses for men,women and children at a reasonable price range.The dresses are designed in home by fine craftsmen and tailored to perfection.We strive for perfection and ensure that our designs are unique.The fabric that we use and the artworks on the merchandise is inspired by Indian lifestyle and clothing.Also,we have now started designing Sarees,Kurtis,Salwars,Kids' Attire and Jewellery.We are now having an exclusive store at Crossroads,15600 8th Street, Bellevue,WA 98008.

Our range of products include exclusive designs,selected products from market catalogs,handloom varieties, jewelry,accessories etc.Our aim is to provide a satisfying shopping experience to all our customers.We believe customer satisfaction is most important and we always put our best efforts to meet our customer needs.Come,visit us to experience a true Indian shopping experience.
